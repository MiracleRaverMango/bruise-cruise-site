Melodrama on Vinyl

Bruise Cruise is your favorite emotional shipwreck—founded in a bedroom with a busted cassette deck and a heart full of eyeliner.

Built from motel poetry, fake IDs, and a little mascara magic, we write songs for breakups that never ended and hotel pools that never closed.
